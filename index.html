WHAT IS A BUSINESS ANALYST?

A Business Analyst (BA) is the bridge between business stakeholders and technical teams. They understand what the business needs, translate those needs into requirements, and ensure solutions actually solve the right problems.

🎯
WHAT THEY DO
  • Gather & document requirements
  • Analyse business processes
  • Map stakeholder needs to solutions
  • Write user stories & use cases
  • Facilitate workshops & meetings
  • Validate solutions before delivery
🏢
WHERE THEY WORK
  • IT & Software companies
  • Banking & Financial services
  • Consulting firms (Big 4, MBB)
  • E-commerce & Retail
  • Healthcare & Pharma
  • Government & Public sector
📈
CAREER GROWTH
  • Junior BA → BA → Senior BA
  • Lead BA / Principal BA
  • Product Manager / Owner
  • Solutions Architect
  • Management Consultant
  • Chief of Staff / COO
